We, the CDC, have embarked on a mission to help communities around the Rochester area struggling with food insecurities. In America, according to the RTS*, an approximate 130 billion meals are wasted each year. Instead of feeding the landfills, these meals could go to people in need. Our purpose is to source crowdfunding and provide substantial and equitable food necessities to all in need.

Our targets are food businesses that throw away foods in good condition. We will form relationships with businesses who are willing to donate these foods. We will hire people to drive food trucks around Rochester picking up those unused foods and catering them to the public. Our goal is to decrease hunger while simultaneously decreasing unnecessary food waste.

We started in 1975 and have served the residents of Rochester, NY since then. We are still ran under the same 3 founders.
